EMPLOYMENT AGREEMENT

This Employment Agreement (the "Agreement") is made this 5th day of February, 2026, by and between TechCorp Solutions Pvt Ltd (the "Company") and Rahul Sharma (the "Employee").

1. POSITION AND DUTIES
The Company agrees to employ the Employee as Senior Developer.

2. COMPENSATION
The Company shall pay the Employee a salary of INR 12,00,000 per annum.

3. NON-COMPETE (High Risk)
During the term of this Agreement and for a period of 5 years after termination, the Employee shall not engage in any business, directly or indirectly, that competes with the Company anywhere in Asia.

4. IP ASSIGNMENT
All intellectual property created by the Employee, whether during work hours or not, shall belong exclusively to the Company.

5. TERMINATION
The Company may terminate this Agreement at any time without notice or cause. The Employee must provide 90 days notice.

6. GOVERNING LAW
This Agreement shall be governed by the laws of India.
